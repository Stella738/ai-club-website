FAQ frequently asked question

Q: Do I need to know the TTC ?

A: Not at all! We meet on campus, and the organizers will guide the group the whole way.

Q: How do I join the club?

A: Just show up to an event! Visit our Discord or Instagram for the sign-up form.

Q: Do I have to pay to join or attend events?

A: Most of our events are free! Sometimes there might be a small fee to cover food or tickets, but we always let you know in advance.
